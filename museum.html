Ellora Caves

Ellora Caves is a historic coastal town in Tamil Nadu, India, renowned for its remarkable rock-cut monuments and temples. These UNESCO World Heritage Sites, including the Shore Temple and the Descent of the Ganges, reveal the artistic brilliance of the Pallava dynasty. Carved directly from granite, the sculptures depict intricate mythological narratives and are a testament to the ingenuity of ancient Indian engineers.
